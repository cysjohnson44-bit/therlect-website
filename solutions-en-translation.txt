SOLUTIONS PAGE - ENGLISH TRANSLATION

Process Steps:

1. Concept & Design
   Title: "Concept & Design"
   Short Desc: "Conduct preliminary thermal assessment based on customer requirements and propose multiple viable heat dissipation solutions."
   Full Desc: "In the early project phase, we conduct in-depth analysis of your product characteristics, application scenarios, and performance requirements. Through detailed thermal assessment, we identify potential thermal management bottlenecks. We consider space constraints, budget, manufacturing processes, and performance objectives to develop multiple viable solutions for you. Each solution undergoes preliminary verification to ensure technical feasibility and economic viability. Our design team will work closely with you to select the optimal solution as the foundation for subsequent development."

2. Simulation & Analysis
   Title: "Simulation & Analysis"
   Short Desc: "Utilize professional software for detailed thermal simulation and structural analysis to predict potential issues."
   Full Desc: "Using industry-leading simulation software such as ANSYS and FloTHERM, we conduct multi-physics coupled analysis. Through 3D thermal flow analysis (CFD), we precisely predict temperature distribution, flow field characteristics, and thermal stress. Combined with structural finite element analysis (FEA), we assess material stress and deformation. We comprehensively validate designs before tooling, significantly reducing trial-and-error costs and development cycles. Simulation results include detailed reports and visualization analysis, providing scientific basis for subsequent decision-making."

3. Prototype Development
   Title: "Prototype Development"
   Short Desc: "Rapidly develop functional prototypes and conduct real-world testing to verify result accuracy."
   Full Desc: "Based on optimized design, we rapidly develop functional prototypes for experimental verification. Using precision testing equipment (infrared thermal imaging cameras, temperature sensor arrays), we collect temperature data under real operating conditions. We compare simulation results with measured data to verify model accuracy. If deviations are found, we conduct root cause analysis and optimize model parameters. Prototype testing covers various conditions including ambient, high, and low temperatures to ensure stable product performance across the full operating range."

4. Production & Manufacturing
   Title: "Production & Manufacturing"
   Short Desc: "Integrate supply chain resources to provide high-quality, high-efficiency mass production services."
   Full Desc: "We establish long-term partnerships with premium global suppliers to ensure stable raw material supply. From precision mold development, injection molding, and mechanical machining to assembly and testing, every step follows strict quality standards. We employ advanced automation equipment and processes to enhance production efficiency and product consistency. We establish comprehensive logistics systems to ensure timely delivery. We can provide flexible production solutions based on order volume, from small-batch trials to large-scale mass production."

5. Quality & Optimization
   Title: "Quality & Optimization"
   Short Desc: "Continuously track product quality and optimize design based on market feedback."
   Full Desc: "After product launch, we establish comprehensive quality tracking mechanisms. Through customer feedback, field testing, and long-term reliability verification, we identify improvement opportunities. Based on actual operational data and market demand changes, we conduct continuous design optimization. Optimization includes material selection, process improvements, cost reduction, and performance enhancement. We are committed to pursuing excellence, ensuring products maintain optimal performance and competitiveness throughout their entire lifecycle. We provide regular technical support and upgrade solutions."
