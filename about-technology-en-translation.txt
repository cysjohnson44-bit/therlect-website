TECHNOLOGY DETAILS - PASSIVE COOLING PAGE

Title: "Passive Cooling Solutions"
Subtitle: "Efficient thermal dissipation without active power consumption"

Introduction:
"Passive cooling represents the most elegant thermal management solution. By optimizing structural design and material selection, we achieve efficient heat dissipation without any active power consumption, resulting in completely silent and maintenance-free operation."

Key Technologies:

1. Heat Sink Design Optimization
Description: "Through advanced CFD simulation, we optimize heat sink geometry, fin spacing, and material distribution to maximize thermal conductivity and heat dissipation efficiency. Our designs can achieve thermal resistance values as low as 0.1°C/W."

2. Thermal Conductive Materials
Description: "We select and apply high-performance thermal conductive materials including aluminum alloys, copper alloys, and advanced composite materials. Each material is selected based on specific application requirements, balancing thermal conductivity, weight, cost, and manufacturability."

3. Natural Convection Optimization
Description: "We leverage natural convection principles to design thermal modules that require no external power. Through careful analysis of airflow patterns and heat transfer mechanisms, we ensure optimal thermal performance in various orientations and environments."

4. Phase-Change Materials (PCM)
Description: "We integrate phase-change materials to provide thermal buffering, absorbing excess heat during peak loads and releasing it during idle periods, maintaining stable operating temperatures."

Applications:
• LED lighting fixtures
• Power supplies and adapters
• Audio amplifiers
• Medical monitoring devices
• Industrial control systems
• Telecommunications equipment

TECHNOLOGY DETAILS - ACTIVE COOLING PAGE

Title: "Active Cooling Solutions"
Subtitle: "High-performance thermal dissipation for demanding applications"

Introduction:
"Active cooling technology represents the frontier of thermal management. By combining advanced cooling systems with intelligent control, we enable devices to operate at peak performance even under extreme thermal loads."

Key Technologies:

1. Air Cooling Systems
Description: "Our advanced fan designs and airflow optimization techniques achieve high cooling efficiency with minimal noise. We utilize variable-speed fans controlled by intelligent algorithms to balance performance and energy consumption."

2. Liquid Cooling Systems
Description: "For ultra-high-power applications, we provide custom liquid cooling solutions. Our designs feature optimized fluid dynamics, precision pump control, and advanced heat exchangers to achieve exceptional thermal performance."

3. Hybrid Cooling Solutions
Description: "We combine air and liquid cooling technologies to create hybrid systems that optimize performance, efficiency, and reliability. These solutions are particularly effective for applications with varying thermal loads."

4. Intelligent Temperature Control
Description: "Our thermal management systems integrate real-time temperature monitoring and predictive algorithms to automatically adjust cooling intensity, ensuring optimal performance while minimizing energy consumption."

5. Thermal Interface Materials (TIM)
Description: "We develop and apply advanced thermal interface materials including thermal pads, thermal greases, and phase-change materials to maximize heat transfer efficiency between components and cooling systems."

Applications:
